Who We Are

Valley DayCare Group is a family-focused childcare organization dedicated to providing safe, nurturing, and enriching environments where children can learn, grow, and thrive.

Through our network of childcare centres, we support families by delivering high-quality early learning programs, experienced educators, and a strong commitment to child development.

Our Mission

To provide exceptional childcare and early learning experiences that foster confidence, creativity, independence, and lifelong learning.

Our Vision

To be a trusted leader in early childhood education, empowering children to reach their full potential while supporting families and strengthening communities.

Valley DayCare Group Story

Valley DayCare Group was founded with a simple goal: to create childcare centres where children feel safe, supported, and inspired every day.

What began as a commitment to serving local families has grown into a trusted network of childcare centres across Alberta. Throughout our growth, our focus has remained the same — providing exceptional care, meaningful learning opportunities, and strong support for families.

Today, Valley DayCare Group continues to invest in quality programs, dedicated educators, and welcoming environments that help children build the foundation for lifelong success.

Our Philosophy

At Valley Day Care Group , we believe every child deserves a safe, nurturing, and inspiring environment where they can learn, grow, and thrive. Across all of our childcare centers, we are committed to providing high-quality early learning experiences that foster curiosity, creativity, confidence, and a lifelong love of learning.

Through hands-on experiences, creativity, and exploration, we help children build confidence, follow their interests, and develop with curiosity and joy.
