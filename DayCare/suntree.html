About Suntree Daycare & OSC

Suntree Daycare and OSC is a not-for-profit childcare organization established to support children, families, and the wider community. The organization’s primary purpose is to provide safe, affordable, and high-quality childcare and early learning services that promote children’s healthy development, education, and well-being.

The organization does not operate for profit or commercial gain. All revenue generated is fully reinvested into childcare programs, facility improvements, educational resources, and staff training. No income is distributed for private benefit, confirming its genuine not-for-profit and community-serving nature.

Charitable & Community Benefit

Suntree Daycare and OSC operates with a clear mandate to advance early childhood education and provide essential social services to families.

  • Advancement of education through structured early learning programs
  • Support to families by providing reliable childcare
  • Contribution to community well-being by enabling parents to work, study, or train

Through these services, the organization contributes directly to Canada’s social and economic infrastructure by supporting workforce participation and early childhood development.
