About Our Daycares

Where children learn, grow, and shine 🌱

Suntree Daycare & OSC

Suntree Daycare and OSC is a not-for-profit childcare organization established to support children, families, and the wider community. The organization’s primary purpose is to provide safe, affordable, and high-quality childcare and early learning services that promote children’s healthy development, education, and well-being.

The organization does not operate for profit or commercial gain. All revenue generated is fully reinvested into childcare programs, facility improvements, educational resources, and staff training. No income is distributed for private benefit, confirming its genuine not-for-profit and community-serving nature.

Charitable & Community Benefit

Suntree Daycare and OSC operates with a clear mandate to advance early childhood education and provide essential social services to families.

  • Advancement of education through structured early learning programs
  • Support to families by providing reliable childcare
  • Contribution to community well-being by enabling parents to work, study, or train

Through these services, the organization contributes directly to Canada’s social and economic infrastructure by supporting workforce participation and early childhood development.

Beaumont Stars Academy

Beaumont Stars Academy is a not-for-profit childcare organization dedicated to supporting children, families, and the community. Its mission is to provide safe, affordable, and high-quality childcare that promotes children’s development, learning, and well-being.

The organization operates solely for community benefit, not profit. All revenue from its daycare and out-of-school care programs is reinvested into services, facilities, learning resources, and staff development, ensuring continuous improvement and support for children and families.

Charitable & Community Benefit

Beaumont Stars Academy is dedicated to advancing early childhood education and supporting families through essential services. Its work aligns with key charitable purposes, including:

  • Providing structured early learning programs to support children’s development
  • Offering reliable childcare to assist families
  • Promoting community well-being by enabling parents to work, study, or train

Through these efforts, the organization contributes to early childhood development and supports Canada’s social and economic well-being.

Kids Valley Daycare

Kids Valley Daycare is committed to helping every child grow, learn, and thrive in a joyful and supportive environment. Through creativity, exploration, and play-based learning, we provide a safe space where children can build confidence and discover their potential.

We value each child’s individuality and tailor our care to meet their unique needs, interests, and abilities. Our qualified staff foster meaningful connections and encourage curiosity, respect, and positive self-esteem. We believe children learn best when they feel safe, supported, and free to explore. Our centers promote inclusivity, respect for diversity, and equal opportunities for all children. By creating a warm, home-like atmosphere and maintaining open communication with families, we aim to build strong relationships that support every child’s development and well-being.

Our Philosophy

At Kidsvalley Daycare & OSC, every child is encouraged to learn, laugh, share, and grow in a nurturing environment. We recognize that each child is unique, with their own strengths and pace of development, and support them through our Flight-Based Curriculum.

Through hands-on experiences, creativity, and exploration, we help children build confidence, follow their interests, and develop with curiosity and joy.
