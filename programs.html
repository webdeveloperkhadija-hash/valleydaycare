Our Programs

Designed for every stage of your child’s growth

👶 Infants Program (0–19 Months)

A Safe, Loving Start for Your Baby

We provide a warm, nurturing environment where your baby feels safe, secure, and loved. Our caregivers focus on gentle routines and responsive care to support emotional bonding and early development.

  • Safe and hygienic environment
  • Gentle daily routines
  • Sensory play and early stimulation
  • Emotional bonding and care
  • Individual attention

Goal: A peaceful space for growth, comfort, and exploration.

🚼 Toddler Town (19 Months – 3 Years)

Where Curiosity Begins

We create an exciting environment where toddlers explore, learn, and grow through play. This stage focuses on discovery, independence, and early communication.

  • Play-based learning
  • Social skill development
  • Interactive exploration
  • Safe, engaging space
  • Guided independence

Goal: Build confidence through exploration and fun.

🎨 Learning Kiddos (Preschool)

Building Confidence Through Learning

Through storytelling, creative play, and hands-on activities, children develop communication, creativity, and early academic skills.

  • Early literacy skills
  • Creative play & imagination
  • Teamwork & interaction
  • Problem-solving
  • Confidence building

Goal: Prepare children for school with confidence.

☀️ Sunshine Room (Pre-K 4–5 Years)

Preparing for School with Confidence

We combine structured learning and play to help children transition smoothly into school while developing independence and responsibility.

  • School readiness
  • Structured + play learning
  • Problem-solving
  • Social development
  • Confidence building

Goal: Ready and excited for kindergarten.

🚀 Kindergarten & OSC (5–12 Years)

Learning Beyond the Classroom

We provide a safe and engaging environment where children grow academically and socially beyond school hours.

  • Homework support
  • Creative activities
  • Outdoor play
  • Friendships & social growth
  • Safe supervision

Goal: Growth, learning, and happiness beyond school.
